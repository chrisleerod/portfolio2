About Me


Hello, my name is Chris Rodriguez, and thank you for stopping by and visiting my portfolio. I am a self-taught designer based in Nashville, Tennessee, attending Belmont University. I thoroughly enjoy technology and the amazing things it is allowing us to do to better humanity and improve our lives.


I own a design agency that creates robust animations, branding identities, UI/UX, web, and other design projects. I have had the privilege to work with over 300 clients.


An interesting thing about myself is that I initially wanted to pursue medicine as my career, but later realized my life-long love for design, computers, and technology was calling my name. This is partly due to the creation of my design agency, imagecrate.
