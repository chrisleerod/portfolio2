Nebula UI Design

2019

Nebula is an automated software company that is growing in the streetwear fashion and sneaker space. It allows its users to automate the checkout process to purchase items and increase their chances on successfully obtaining limited shoes, clothing, accessories, and other highly sought-after items. These items can be sold for an after-market price that allows the owner to gain profit margins that may seem other-worldly to the average person.

This project was a commercial project that I was personally hired to do. The purpose was to give their current UI a refreshing update and feature a number of new updates that would be coming to the software.


Roles: Product Design, UX

Software Used: Sketch , Adobe Illustrator

The Project


This project had one goal: make a lasting impression on Cybersole's nearly 100,000 followers and general audience. The animation itself draws inspiration from a popular style called "Kinetic Typography," which involves meaningful cuts and motion that utilize the soundtrack to enhance the visuals of the animation itself.


This was a big opportunity not only for myself but for the company to make an everlasting impression in the community and audience that were intended to watch. I pulled inspiration from Apple, Google, and Microsoft to study and see well-done, perfectly executed, and marketable animations or videos.


What exactly makes up a successful ad or marketing animation? Is it the animation style? Is it the color scheme that's carefully chosen and kept uniform throughout? Or maybe it's a little of everything. This is something Cybersole and I wanted. Something that just looked and felt right. This took hours upon hours looking at examples, trial and error, and suggestions.
